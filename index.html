Frequently Asked Questions

How do you calculate exact age?

To calculate exact age, subtract your birth year from the current year, then adjust for months and days. Our age calculator does this automatically, giving you precise age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes.

What is my age if I was born in 1990?

If you were born in 1990, your age depends on the current year and your birth month/day. Use our age calculator to enter your exact birth date and get your precise age in years, months, and days.

How many days until my next birthday?

Enter your birth date into our calculator, and it will automatically calculate exactly how many days remain until your next birthday. It also shows your next birthday date.

Is this age calculator accurate?

Yes, our age calculator is highly accurate. It accounts for leap years and different month lengths to give you the exact number of years, months, days, hours, and minutes between your birth date and today.
